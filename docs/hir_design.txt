HIR STRUCTURES
--------------
- Declarations will define Hir nodes tied to scoped hash-tables and HirIdentifierId's will be the paths locating those definied nodes.
- Usages of any identifiers will explore those paths to ensure the existence of any mentioned identifiers by walking the scope tree and 
  then storing the HirId's at those the referenced Strings inside an HirIdentifierId.
- Declarations much check their local scope to ensure no redefinitions occur.

(declarations)
insert a String representing the name (none-scoped newly bound identifier) into the Map of the local HirScope

(usages, used in types and exprs)
HirIdentifier (eg. std..println):
    []HirId -> perform scoped-aware String look-up -> HirId (perform a hash look-up to tie the HirSymbolId's to store a fixed HirId)

HirId ->
    | HirDeclaration:
        | HirModule -> []HirId
        | HirFunctionDecl:
            name: Span
            params: Params
            body: HirBody
            HirBody?
        | HirDestructorDecl:
            HirFunctionDecl
        | HirExternCFunctionDecl
        | HirVarDecl
        | HirStructDecl
        | HirVariantDecl
        | HirUnionDecl
        | HirContractDecl
        | HirDefTypeDecl
    | HirType: 
        | HirTypeBuiltin
        | HirStruct
        | HirVariant
        | HirUnion
    | HirStatement:
        | HirBlockStmt:
            block: HirBody
        | HirExprStmt
        | HirEmptyStmt
        | HirBreakStmt
        | HirIfStmt
        | HirElseStmt
        | HirLoopStmt
        | HirReturnStmt
        | HirYieldStmt
    | HirExpr
    | HirIdentifier:
        id: HirId
    | HirBody (executable):
        nodes: []HirId
